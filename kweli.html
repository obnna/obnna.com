Front end web developer

@kweliTV

KweliTV is a startup video streaming platform that is looking to change the media. Kwelitv is similiar to Netflix and Hulu, but it is entirely composed of films made by members of the African diaspora. As of today, the platform hosts 270 films by over 100 filmmakers from all around the world, half of which are women. The content is highly curated, with over 98% of films having been featured in international film festivals and more than half having won awards.We curate and create independent films, social impact documentaries, web shows and news that culturally show a true reflection of misrepresented communities of color globally.

I had the great opportunity to work for kweliTV as a front end web developer. Over the course of 2 years, I and my fellow developer created kweliTV from scratch. We replicated mock images given to us by the design team using JavaScript/jQuery, CSS, HTML, Sengrid, AWS for cloud hosting, and postgreSQL.

At KweliTV, we utlized the agile web developement strategy, in which we (developers) meet every 2 weeks with the design team to construct a project plan. We remain in touch with other developers using slack to post quick questions, git for version control and jira for tracking bugs.I am excited for the slated website launch sometime in August 2017
